For Him

I'm your Servant

  • Dear God,

First of all, I am humbling myself in front of you. No words can describe how good You are. Without you, I am nothing and with you, I can be everything.

Your love is so deep and I know that you are always watching over me. You are a forgiving God and a beautiful Savior. You even sacrificed Your only begotten Son, just to save me, a mortal sinner that can't even justify my own sins.

With this letter, I am expressing my love for You, though I know that this is not enough, this is one of many ways to express how thankful I am to have you with my side.

God, I want to be a faithful servant for you. Though I know that I'm not as good as other people may be, I just want you to know that I can serve for You as much as you want me to serve You.

I want my life to be more of You and less of me. And I promise You that I won't compromise, ever again, for the love of You.

I love You and I want to be a deserving servant who can celebrate with Your victory.

Amen.
